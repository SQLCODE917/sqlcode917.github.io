# Profession

## 業[业] ye(4)

Tree **木** and the foliage **丵** it produces.  
(n) industry, profession, occupation, estate

- **木** mu(4) - Pictograph of a tree.
(n) tree, wood
- **丵** zhuo(2) - Pictograph of branches and foliage.

------

## 業

kun: わざ  
on: ギョウ, ゴウ  
(n) business, vocation, arts, performance
