# To See

## 見[见] jian(4)

Eye **目** above a standing person **儿**.  
(v) to see, to meet  
(n) view, opinion

- **目** mu(4) - Pictograph of an eye.
(n) eye, category
- **儿** ren(2) - Pictograph of human legs.
(n) human, person
