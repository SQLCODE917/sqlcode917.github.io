# Walk

## 行 xing(2) hang(2)

Step **彳** and stop **亍**.
Also explained as right step and left step.  
**xing(2)**:  
(v) to walk, to do  
(adj) alright, O.K., capable  
**hang(2)**:
(n) line, row, company/firm, line of business/profession

- **彳** chi(4) - Pictograph showing a person taking a small step.
(n) step, left step

- **亍** chu(4) - Reverse of step **彳**.
(n) stop, right step
