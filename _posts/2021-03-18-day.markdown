# Day

## 日 ri(4)

Pictogram of the Sun. A dot or line was added in the centre to distinguish it from visually similar characters, e.g. **囗** (early form of **丁**).  
(n) the Sun  
(n) day


______

## 日

kun: ひ, -び, -か  
on: ニチ, ジツ

day, sun, Japan, counter for days
