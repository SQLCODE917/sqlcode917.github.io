# East

## 東[东] dong(1)

Sun **日** rising behind a tree **木**.  
(n) East, host, owner

- **日** ri(4) - Pictogram of the Sun.
A dot or line was added in the centre to distinguish it from visually similar characters, e.g. **囗** (early form of **丁**).
(n) the Sun, day
- **木** mu(4) - Pictograph of a tree.
(n) tree, wood
