# Middle

## 中 zhong(1) zhong(4)

(n) center, middle  
(preposition) among  
(adj) medium  
(v) strike, hit (a target), to be struck  

- **丨** gen(3) - Ideograph representing vertical things  
- **口** wei(2) - Ideograph representing an enclosure
